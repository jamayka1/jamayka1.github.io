OUR COMMUNITY

Stacknet is a shared Egyptian company founded in August 2018 as a provider of new service in the medical insurance market ,working business to business supporting our two customers (Healthcare companies & service providers)as a communication channel between them and providing more packages of services in order to enhance the image of medical insurance.

Show vedio
  • The market problem (pain) we are solving:
    1. We are solving the problem of miscommunication , fragmentation , extending , dissatisfaction and poor quality of medical insurance
  • The affected:
    1. The healthcare companies (25).
    2. the service providers (5000) inside market.
    3. Approximately (15000) willing to enter the market.
    4. The end user (patients) who is our main target.
  • The service delivered:
    1. Medical network management for service providers (technically & financially)
    2. Making a supervision for the exist Medical networks.
    3. Making new medical networks for the Medicare comps
    4. Service one is already beneficial for the customer 2 (Medicare companies ) who get it for free.
    5. The service of quality or the case management that is presenting to the Medicare companies to keep their exist clients and attract new ones and to make them satisfied.
  • Solution to the problem:
    1. We are working as a communication channel between our two customers and shortcutting each side of them in front of the other using a different two needed packages of services.
